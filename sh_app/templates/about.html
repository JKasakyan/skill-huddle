About Skill Huddle

SH is a web application that provides a way to find and manage in-person meetings (called Huddles) between nearby people who have a shared interest in learning specific skills.

Definitions

  • Skill Huddle (SH) - The Skill Huddle web application.
  • Non-Registered User (NRU) - A visitor who does not have an SH account.
  • SH User (SHU) - A visitor who does have an SH account.
  • League - A community of people who meet together.
  • Suggestion - A proposal for a Huddle that is voted on by League Members.
  • Huddle - A meeting among League Members.
  • League Member (LM) - An SHU that belongs to a specific League.
  • Official - A skilled League Member who leads Huddles.
  • Head Official (HO) - The managing Official of a League.
  • Admin - A SH staffer who uses their unrestricted permissions to enforce SH policies.
